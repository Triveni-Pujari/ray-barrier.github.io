Welcome to Ray-Barrier

Drive with Shine and Protection

Ray-Barrier is a Multi Function Film that acts as Ultra-Violet Rays and Heat Rays Screen. TO effectively protect you from the sun, window films need to reject Ultra-Violet rays and Near Infra-Red rays, while allowing Far Infra-Red and as much Visible Light through as possible. This intelligent filtering, made possible in Ray-Barrier through nanotechnology, is known as total solar heat rejection, an industry measurement recommended by the International Window Film Association (IWFA)

Just applying Ray-Barrier film to your car window glass reduces the heat in the car by providing comfort to you and your family.
